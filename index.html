Department of Lost Circuits

a generative postage series for obsolete electronics

Controls

  • S Save current sheet as PNG
  • R Regenerate with new random seed
  • ← → Navigate between sheets

Each stamp is generated from device data including release year, category, and image.

Year affects circuit density — older devices (1960s) show more complex circuitry.

Category determines badge — audio, storage, display, computing, gaming.

Border style is randomized — perforation, scalloped, or zigzag edges.
